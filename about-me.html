About Me

I am Sahil Parde, a leading fitness educator with the best selling course on fitness and nutrition. Trusted by 400 students and counting

No Guidance

My passion for fitness started when i was pursuing my engineering, i was skinny as a stick. I started training in a local gym in my neighbourhood, there was just one trainer who had zero understanding about exercise and nutrition. He couldn't guide me or anyone else for that matter.

Skinny and under-confident

I was weighing 48 Kgs when I started and after a year I was weighing 50 Kgs !!! Yes that's right I gained just 2 Kgs in a Whole year. I was pissed at myself and with no guidance whatsoever I decided to take the bull by the horns. I started investing into learning about exercise and nutrition science. I started to train hard and apply things i was learning, after some time is started seeing results.

Life of a Personal Trainer

I wanted to turn my passion into a career, I wanted to help people who were just like me, who did not have the guidance, so I started working as a Floor Trainer in the same gym I used to train. The reason I worked as a floor trainer and not as a personal trainer is because there was no trend of personal training in that gym. Just a couple of clients every year and that's it. I wanted to help people but I also wanted to build my career and with a salary of INR 8,000/-

My struggles

I could barely cover my expenses. I used all the knowledge i gained and started to apply and voila!! I got my first Personal Training client. Within a few weeks I started the trend of personal training in that gym and other coaches as got inspired too. Few years down the line i also started my online fitness coaching company, simultaneously I also helped other coaches kick start their personal training and coaching business

Life of a Personal Trainer

After sharing words with all the coaches there was this one thing that came to my knowledge which even i was a victim of when I started investing in my education. "Personal Training courses are really expensive". There are only a couple of courses that I know are actually worthy of what they ask for. Personal Trainers have to pay a lot of money to get themselves educated only to unlearn all the misinformation that they have learned in the courses.

My mission

For a Trainer who has a basic salary of INR 8-15k. It is really hard to pay the huge fees which these institute ask for. To give you an idea the top fitness academy charges INR 1.2 lac for their CPT and Nutrition course.
This is the reason I started IENS, Institute of Exercise and Nutrition Sciences to help trainers educate themselves and a make a name for themselves in the fitness industry at an affordable price
