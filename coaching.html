How does our online coaching work?

Initial call and onboarding

To begin with, we have a five-fold assessment procedure which enables us to customize your nutrition and exercise program to your current state of health, eating patterns and lifestyle.
‍
a. Two digital forms filled by you which determine your current exercise and nutrition habits, your goals and any medical symptoms you may be experiencing.
‍
b. A 4-day food record in which you keep a food diary on 2 weekdays and a weekend to enable us to identify strengths and weaknesses in your current food habits.
‍
c. A photographic assessment in which we ask you to send us specific pictures of your physique to ascertain its strengths and weaknesses and help us write the best exercise program to address your goals. Any postural deficits identified will also be corrected through exercise.
Nutrition and workout plan

This data is then used to design a customized, well rounded, and sustainable exercise and nutritional regimen which is sent to you in a PDF document.

This document also details warm up and cool down routines, rest intervals to be taken between exercises, instructions on how to track progress, a supplementation regimen keeping your goals and budget in mind and gym gear you would need to get started.
Constant support and guidance

1. Customized Exercise programs updated weekly
2. Tailored Nutrition programs updated weekly
3. Not weekly but daily check-ins
4. Unlimited WhatsApp support
5. Unlimited Call support
